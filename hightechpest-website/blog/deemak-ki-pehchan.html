Deemak Ki Pehchan Kaise Karein?
7 Warning Signs Jo Aapko Zarur Jaanne Chahiye

✍️ Mohd Kaleem  ·  📅 June 2025  ·  ⏱️ 5 min read

Deemak (Termite) ek aisi khamoshi se kaam karne wali problem hai jo aapke ghar ki neenv ko andar hi andar khokhla karti rehti hai — aur aapko pata bhi nahi chalta. Jab tak nuqsan dikhai deta hai, tab tak lakho rupaye ki lakdi, furniture aur structure barbaad ho chuki hoti hai.

Isiliye zaroori hai ki deemak ki shuruati pehchan karna aaye. Yahan hain 7 aisi warning signs jo aapko kabhi bhi ignore nahi karni chahiye:

01

Hollow Sound Aana Lakdi Thakthakane Par

Agar aap apni wooden door frame, cupboard ya wall ko thakthakate hain aur awaaz khaali ya hollow lage — yeh deemak ka sabse pehla aur clear sign hai. Deemak andar hi andar lakdi kha leti hai lekin upar ki surface chodh deti hai.

02

Paint Mein Bubbles Ya Ukharna

Agar paint bina kisi nami ke bubble bana raha hai ya ukhad raha hai, toh yeh deemak ki galiyaron (tunnels) mein nami ki wajah se hota hai. Aksar log ise water leakage samajh lete hain — galti mat kijiye.

03

Mud Tubes Deewaron Ya Foundation Par

Subterranean termites mitti ke patale nali (mud tubes) banate hain jo pencil ki moti hoti hain. Yeh tubes zameen se upar ki taraf jaati hain. Yeh sabse reliable sign hai ki deemak active hain.

04

Darawaaze Ya Khidkiyaan Tight Ho Jaana

Agar pehle se aasani se khulne wale darawaaze ya windows achanak tight ya stuck hone lage hain — yeh deemak ki wajah se wood mein nami aur warping ka sign ho sakta hai.

05

Tiny Holes in Wood (Pinholes)

Laki mein chhote-chhote sukhi mitti se bhare holes dikhen — yeh drywood termites ka kaam hota hai. Aksar furniture ya purani lakdi mein yeh holes dikhai dete hain.

06

Termite Droppings (Frass)

Darawazon ke neeche ya furniture ke paas kaali ya gehri bhoori rang ki sand jaise daane dikhein toh yeh deemak ki potty (frass) hoti hai. Iske milne par turant inspection karwayein.

07

Udte Huye Deemak (Swarming)

Barish ke mausam mein ya usके baad agar aapko choti chiti jaise pankh wale keede ghar mein ya ghar ke bahar dikhen — yeh swarmers hain. Yeh naye termite colony banate hain. Turant action lo!
