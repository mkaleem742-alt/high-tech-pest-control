Monsoon Mein Pest Control Kyun Zaroori Hai?
5 Khatre Jo Aapko Jaanne Chahiye

✍️ Mohd Kaleem  ·  📅 July 2025  ·  ⏱️ 4 min read

Lucknow mein barish ka mausam aate hi ek cheez aur bhi badh jaati hai — pests. July se September tak Uttar Pradesh ke ghar cockroach, mosquito, chuhe aur deemak ki activity mein badi tezi aati hai. Yeh koi coincidence nahi hai — iska ek scientific reason hai.

Nami, geela maahol aur standing water insects aur rodents ke breeding ke liye perfect conditions create karte hain. Agar aapne monsoon se pehle ya monsoon mein pest control nahi karwayi toh is article ko zaroor padhein.

Monsoon Mein Yeh 5 Pests Sabse Zyada Sataate Hain

🦟

1. Mosquito — Dengue & Malaria Ka Khaatra

Barish ke ruke hue paani mein — coolers, pots, tyres, chhaton par — mosquito eggs 24-48 ghante mein hatch ho jaate hain. Lucknow mein every monsoon dengue cases spike karte hain. Sirf cooler ka paani change karna enough nahi hai — professional fogging zaruri hai.

🪳

2. Cockroach — Humidity Mein 3X Fast Breeding

Cockroach humid conditions mein bahut tezi se reproduce karte hain. Ek female cockroach ek saal mein 300+ eggs de sakti hai — monsoon mein yeh rate aur badh jaata hai. Kitchen drain, geeli diwaaren aur washing area inke favorite breeding spots hain barish mein.

🪲

3. Termite Swarming — Monsoon Ki Shuruat Mein

June-July mein pehli heavy barish ke baad termite swarmers (pankh wale deemak) niklte hain nayi colony banane ke liye. Agar aapke ghar ki kisi bhi deewaron ya lakdi mein yeh dikhe toh turant action lo — colony pehle se exist karti hai.

🐀

4. Rat / Chuhe — Barish Mein Ghar Dhoondhhte Hain

Monsoon mein chuhe bahar ki geeli zameen chhod ke shelter dhoodhte hain — aur aapka ghar unhe perfect lagta hai. Yeh wiring nochte hain, food contaminate karte hain aur diseases spread karte hain. Barish se pehle entry points seal karna best prevention hai.

🐜

5. Ants — Barish Mein Colony Migration

Barish mein ants apni underground colonies chhod ke safe jagah dhoodhte hain — often aapke ghar mein. Kitchen counter, sugar ke dabbe, fruit bowl — inhe sab chahiye. Pre-monsoon ant treatment se yeh migration rok sakte hain.

Monsoon Mein Khud Kya Karein — 7 Tips

  • Cooler, bucket, flower pots mein paani ruka na rehne do — har 2-3 din mein change karein
  • Kitchen sink ka drain raat ko plug karein — cockroach is raste andar aate hain
  • Window aur door frames ke cracks silicon sealant se band karein
  • Geela kapda ya mop raat ko khula mat chhodo — pest attract hota hai
  • Angan ya roof par paani jama na hone do — mosquito breeding ground
  • Grocery bags bahar se check karein pehle — ants ya roaches aa sakte hain
  • Rat entry points (pipe holes, AC holes) steel wool ya cement se band karein

💡 Pro Tip: Pre-Monsoon Treatment Sabse Best Hai

June mein — barish shuru hone se pehle — pest control karwana sabse zyada effective hai. Is time treatment karwane se chemicals settle ho jaate hain aur puri monsoon mein protection milti hai. Last minute mat karo.

Kab Professional Help Zaruri Hai?

Ghar par jugaad aur repellents temporary relief dete hain — pest colony ko nahi marte. Agar aap in mein se koi bhi experience kar rahe hain, professional treatment zaruri hai:

  • Roz raat kitchen mein cockroach dikh rahe hain
  • Body par red bites ke nishaan hain (bed bug)
  • Ceiling ya darazon se sawdust gir raha hai (termite)
  • Rat ki droppings dikh rahi hain kitchen ya storeroom mein
  • Mosquito repellent se bhi mosquito nahi ja raha
