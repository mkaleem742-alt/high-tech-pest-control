Kaunsa Termite Treatment Better Hai?

Jab bhi koi humse poochta hai "deemak ke liye best treatment kya hai?" — hamaara pehla sawaal hota hai: "Ghar naya ban raha hai ya pehle se bana hua hai?"

Yeh ek bahut zaroori distinction hai. Dono treatments — Porous Pipe System aur Post Construction Drilling — apni jagah bilkul sahi hain. Lekin ek galat jagah use karna matlab waste of money. Is article mein hum dono ka complete comparison karenge.

Pehle Samjhein — Dono Kaam Kaise Karte Hain

🔩

Porous Pipe System

  • 📌 Construction ke waqt slab se pehle pipe daali jaati hai
  • 📌 Pipe soil mein chemical slowly release karti hai
  • 📌 Bahar ke valve se refill — koi drilling nahi kabhi
  • 📌 HDPE pipe 50+ saal tak kaam karti hai
  • 📌 Initial cost zyada, long-term cost less
VS
🔧

Post Construction Drilling

  • 📌 Bane hue ghar mein holes drill kiye jaate hain
  • 📌 Chemical inject kiya jaata hai — holes seal
  • 📌 Har treatment mein drilling karni padti hai
  • 📌 5-7 saal baad re-treatment zaruri
  • 📌 Initial cost less, recurring cost hoti hai

Kaunsa Choose Karein? Simple Decision Guide

🔩 Porous Pipe Choose Karein Agar…

Aap abhi naya ghar bana rahe hain ya construction planning stage mein hain. PCC ho chuka ho aur RCC slab abhi daalni ho — yahi perfect window hai. Ek baar lagao aur kabhi drilling nahi.

🔧 Drilling Method Choose Karein Agar…

Aapka ghar pehle se bana hua hai — chahe naya ho ya 20 saal purana. Porous pipe ab lag nahi sakti. Drilling hi ek effective option hai existing homes ke liye, aur yeh bilkul effective bhi hai.

Kya Porous Pipe Existing Ghar Mein Lag Sakti Hai?

Yeh sabse common question hai aur simple answer hai: Nahi. Porous pipe ko slab ke neeche daalna padta hai — jo tabhi possible hai jab slab daal hi raha ho. Ek baar slab/floor ban gaye, pipe installation impossible hai. Existing gharon ke liye drilling hi option hai.

Long-Term Cost Calculation — Kaunsa Sasta Padta Hai?

1000 sqft ghar ke liye 20 saal mein:

  • 🔩 Porous Pipe: ₹30,000 (installation) + ₹5,000 × 3 refills = ~₹45,000
  • 🔧 Drilling: ₹6,000 × 4 treatments = ₹24,000 + time + disturbance

Note: Porous pipe mein convenience aur zero disturbance ka bhi value hai. Numbers approximate hain — actual depend karta hai property size aur treatment frequency par.
