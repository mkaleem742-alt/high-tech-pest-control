Kya Hota Hai Yeh?

Porous Pipe Reticulation System — Samajhiye Aasaan Bhasha Mein

Jab aap naya ghar bana rahe hote hain — slab daalne se pehle — zameen mein ek network of perforated (chhede wali) pipes daal di jaati hain. In pipes mein termite-killer chemical bhara hota hai jo dheere dheere soil mein absorb hota rehta hai.

Jab chemical weak padne lage (5-7 saal baad), ek simple valve se bahar se refill ho jaata hai — bina ek bhi tile todne ke, bina ek bhi drill ke.

Yeh system internationally proven hai aur deemak se permanent protection ka sabse smart tarika hai naye construction ke liye.

Porous pipe ke microscopic holes se chemical slowly soil mein absorb hota hai

2️⃣ Slab daal do — pipe neeche rehti hai
3️⃣ 5-7 saal baad — bahar valve se refill

Installation Process

Step-by-Step: Porous Pipe Kaise Lagta Hai?

Humara trained team bilkul sahi samay par aata hai — construction schedule ke saath coordinate karke.

1

Site Survey & Planning

Hamare expert aapke construction site visit karke floor plan ke hisab se pipe layout plan karte hain. Foundation area, column positions sab consider kiya jaata hai.

2

Pipe Network Installation (Before Slab)

PCC (Plain Cement Concrete) ke baad aur RCC slab daalne se pehle — HDPE porous pipes ek network mein bichhaai jaati hain. Refill valve bahar ki side mein set kiya jaata hai.

3

Initial Chemical Fill

Pipes mein ISI mark termiticide chemical bhara jaata hai. Yeh chemical pipe ke microscopic holes se slowly soil mein spread hota hai — ek chemical barrier banata hai.

4

Inspection & Documentation

Puri installation check karne ke baad written guarantee certificate diya jaata hai. Refill schedule aur valve location clearly document kiya jaata hai.

5

Easy Refill — Bina Kisi Disturbance Ke

5-7 saal baad — sirf bahar ke valve se chemical pump karke refill. Na tile tootegi, na drilling hogi, na ghar mein koi disturbance — literally 30 minutes ka kaam.
