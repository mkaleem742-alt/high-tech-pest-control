Deemak (termite) ek aisi samasya hai jo chupchaap aapke ghar ki lakdi, furniture aur structure ko andar se khokhla kar deti hai. Jab tak nuqsan dikhta hai, tab tak lakhon ka damage ho chuka hota hai. Isliye sahi samay par **termite treatment** karana bahut zaroori hai.

Is post mein hum jaanenge ki deemak ko kaise pehchanein aur treatment kab karani chahiye.

## Deemak Pehchanne Ke 7 Tarike

### 1. Hollow Lakdi
Darwaze ya furniture ko thakthakane par khali (hollow) awaaz aaye, to andar deemak ho sakti hai.

### 2. Mud Tubes (Mitti Ki Naliyan)
Deewaron par pencil jaisi patli mitti ki naliyan deemak ki nishani hain.

### 3. Paint Mein Bubbles
Bina paani ke paint phoolna ya ukhadna deemak ka sign hai.

### 4. Tight Darwaze-Khidki
Achanak darwaze ya khidkiyan jam hone lagein.

### 5. Lakdi Ka Powder
Furniture ke paas bareek lakdi ka bura (powder) girna.

### 6. Pankh Wale Keede
Barish ke baad udte hue deemak (swarmers) dikhna.

### 7. Deewar Par Daag
Lakdi ya deewar par bhura ya peela daag aana.

## Termite Treatment Kab Karayein?

In mein se koi bhi nishani dikhte hi turant treatment karani chahiye. Deemak bahut tezi se phailti hai. Sabse achha samay hai barish se pehle (June-July), kyunki is samay deemak sabse zyada active hoti hai.

**Naye Ghar Ke Liye:** Construction ke samay hi anti-termite treatment ya porous pipe system lagwana sabse achha hai.

**Bane Hue Ghar Ke Liye:** Drill-Fill-Seal technology se treatment hoti hai, jo deemak ko jad se khatam karti hai.
